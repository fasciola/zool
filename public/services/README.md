# Business Service Images Directory

Place your custom service images in this directory to display them in the **Our Business Services** card section.

## File Naming Convention

Your images should be named sequentially according to the Service ID:

1. **Business Setup & Licensing:** `service-1.jpg`
2. **Trade License Services:** `service-2.jpg`
3. **Professional PRO Services:** `service-3.jpg`
4. **Corporate Banking Assistance:** `service-4.jpg`
5. **Visa & Residency Solutions:** `service-5.jpg`
6. **Office Space Solutions:** `service-6.jpg`
7. **Tax & VAT Advisory:** `service-7.jpg`
8. **Trademark & Brand Protection:** `service-8.jpg`
9. **Local Sponsor Services:** `service-9.jpg`
10. **Golden Visa Consultations:** `service-10.jpg`

---

### Fallback Behavior (Built-in)
You do not need to upload images for all services at once! If an image is missing from this directory, the application automatically falls back to curated high-resolution Unsplash photos seamlessly.
